Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance for weddings and 4-8 weeks for other events. However, we can often accommodate shorter notice for smaller gatherings.

Do you offer tasting sessions?

Yes! We offer complimentary tasting sessions for events with 75+ guests. For smaller events, tasting sessions are available at a nominal fee.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We can create menus for vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Cape Town and surrounding areas including Stellenbosch, Paarl, and the Western Cape. Contact us for events outside these areas.
